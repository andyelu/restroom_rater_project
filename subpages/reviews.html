Our No-Gos

Ina Gittings Building

These restrooms smell EXTREMELY musty (CS Majors PLEASE start taking showers). If you use this, make sure to hold your breath. Also, the all-gender single-user bathrooms aren't sound proof.

Main Library

If privacy is a priority, you probably won't find it at the library's restrooms. They are are huge and pristine, but VERY busy during normal hours. You will be lucky to find an empty all-gender single-user restroom.

Steward Observatory

Very old restrooms. No urinal dividers, and only curtains for stall doors. Yikes.
